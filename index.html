How this works

stuff in, sermons out

stuff

Tender mercies, study insights, half-thoughts. Anything worth writing down. Entries never leave.

questions

The things you’re wrestling with. They can sit for years, gathering notes each time you return.

sermons

String stuff and questions in order — for a talk, a lesson, family night, the youth.

capture

Write it down. You won’t remember it in 30 seconds. WRITE IT DOWN. The tab you’re on says which drawer it lands in — stuff or questions.

tags

Casually organize your mess. Buzzwords of your choice — topic, voice, theme, intended audience. Tap one to filter; the ones you use most rise to the top.

ponder

Tap any entry to open the ponder pad — the line up top, a scratch pad below for scriptures, thoughts, and where it leads. Each keeps its own.

notes

Endless blank space. Free journaling, brain-dumps, whatever doesn’t belong anywhere else.

vault

Buried entries rest here. Want a second look? Dig one up. Ready to let it go? Delete for good from here.
